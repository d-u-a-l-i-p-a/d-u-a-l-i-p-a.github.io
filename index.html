Dua ​Trivia​

  • Dua can solve a Rubik's cube! Can you?
  • Dua Lipa doesn't go by a stage name, her real birth name is Dua Lipa
  • Dua kept a notebook with all 97 songs she wrote for 'Radical Optimism', lyrics, thoughts, and more!
  • 'Future Nostalgia' is inspired by disco music from the 70's and 80's!
  • She's an actor!? Yes! She had her acting debut in 2017 film 'Fifty Shades Darker'
  • Dua's debut self-titled album reached Platinum status in both the U.S. and the U.K.
  • This SNL season the Dua Lipa show is now the most watched episode since Oct. 28, 2023!
  • Dua is a big advocate for mental health awareness and spoke up about her struggles with anxiety
  • Lipa described the process of making 'Radical Optimism' a form of therapy
  • Her debut album was a very personal project, as she co-wrote almost every song!
  • In an Instagram post Dua revealed her second album title with a 'Future Nostalgia' tattoo
  • Dua wrote 97 songs and only kept 11 tracks for the Radical Optimism album!
  • Her first name, 'Dua', means 'love' in Albanian
  • Even though it was the fifth single for her second album, 'Levitating (feat. Dababy)' became a huge hit for her!
  • The second 'Radical Optimism' single, 'Training Season' is about getting ready for something big, possibly a world tour..?
  • Dua worked as a waitress in London at age 15
  • She released her debut single "New Love" in 2015 and followed with her debut studio self-titled album 2 years later
  • Lipa learned the term 'Radical Optimism' from a friend of hers and has been obsessed with it, leading up to her third studio album
  • Her third single for 'Radical Optimism', 'Illusion' hints at the reality of social media, and illusions posted on there...
